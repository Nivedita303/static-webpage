Manufacturing facilities

With perhaps one of the most diverse Manufacturing Facilities, we have complete in house manufacturing facilities to design & Develop products from Pilot Scale Manufacturing to Commercial Scale manufacturing. We have manufactured Enamelled wires of 42 SWG to Fire Survival Cables, to Data Communication Cables to LT Power Cables of 1000 Sqmm.
We have facilities to manufacture any range of cables with our in-house facilities to braid/tape/Tape Sinter/Extrude/enamel/ any thermo polymer. We have multiple Conductor drawing machines to draw any size of Aluminum/Alloy/Copper etc.
With such vast Manufacturing facilities we are perhaps one of the few cable companies to offer complete electric cable range.
The LT cables are specific to the environment and their intended use. With our in-house facilities and our experience of over 40 years we are capable to design, manufacture and provide LT cables for following Sectors:

  • Power/Transmission/Distribution Sector
  • Infrastructure/Building Wires
  • Automotive Sector
  • Telecom Sector
  • Oil & Gas
  • Defence
  • Aerospace

Testing facilities

We believe that cables are a vital infrastructural input and should be of the best possible quality. Our cables are assuredly reliable because we are highly quality conscious. Our In- house lab testing facility helps us monitor the quality standards at every single stage, right from the raw materials to in process and final product. We are a vertically integrated unit with our in house compounding manufacturing unit and copper drawing units hence enabling us to have a stringent control on the quality standards. Apart from ISO Certifications, We are also a ZED Certified Company. We currently hold 4 BIS licenses including IS 694, IS 7098 part 1, IS 1554 Part 1 and IS 14255. We have complete in-housing testing facilities as per various National and International Electric Cable Standards. Apart from Electric Cables, we also have facilities to test Poly Imide Insulated/ Enamelled Wires and Cables.

About Us

Saraswati Cables, a leader in Northern India for specialty wires and cables, established in 1981, having its head office & Factory at the foothills of Shivalik in Distt Una of Himachal Pradesh, India, equipped with state of the art manufacturing plant spread over 6,000 sq mtr area in an environment friendly surrounding.
Saraswati Cables is a family-owned company operating in a globally competitive industry. As owners, we are proud of the company’s legacy and good standing in the community. Saraswati Cables’ success is rooted in hard work, social responsibility and innovation and we believe that sustaining these core values is vital to the company’s future. We are long-term owners and have a long-term perspective too.
We have complete in-house R&D, Manufacturing, and Compounding facilities and hence are cost effective and reliable in terms of performance. The Company is financially strong and growing rapidly year on year with its innovative products. The company’s current production capacity is about 1000 Kms of wires and cables a month of assorted sizes of wires & cables.
Our employees are dedicated to ensuring that the name of Saraswati Cables’ remains a sign of quality and skill.

Managing Director

Sh. Madan Mohan Singh a devout Electrical Engineer who left a promising career in Bhabha Atomic Research Centre, Trombay in Core Reactor Design as a Nuclear Scientist to start his own manufacturing unit of Electrical Cables in his hometown, in dist. Una in Himachal Pradesh in 1982. With his hands-on and frugal engineering approach he has led the unit from the front, from Product Design to Marketing to even Machinery Design. He has been at the core of Saraswati Cables and his humongous experience of over 40 years in the electrical cable industry has been illuminating for our company.
It was because of his engineering/Management skills that the company was able to successfully complete the supply of the complete Data Communication cable of 132 Kms for IRBM Agni Missile, in 1987, as one of the first indigenous manufacturers for the item from India.
His goal of securing the economic and societal goals has stemmed deeply through his own learning and beliefs, as he strives to make the company achieve greater heights in the coming years.
